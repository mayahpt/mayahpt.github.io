Cells Counting by Image Processing

This project belongs to the subject Computer Aided Diagnosis done in the 4th year of my M.Sc in Biomedical Engineer. As part of our university projects, we undertook the task of implementing image processing algorithms in Matlab. Our objectives were: firstly, to discern the region of interest (ROI) within the cell counter image; secondly, to accurately identify cells within the image that conform to predefined rules for cell counting, ensuring they are positioned within the designated ROI; and finally, to quantify and analyze the outcomes produced by our algorithm.
